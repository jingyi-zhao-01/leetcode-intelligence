# 🚀 14-Day LeetCode Plan (2 Hours/Day)

## Daily Structure
- 15 min → Template recall from memory
- 75 min → Solve 3–4 problems
- 20 min → Analyze mistakes and rewrite clean solution
- 10 min → Re-solve 1 weak problem from previous days

Time caps:
- Easy → 10 min
- Medium → 25 min
- Hard → 35 min

## Mandatory Reinforcement Rule
- Re-solve 1 weak problem every day.
- No passive reading.
- Always rewrite final clean solution from scratch.
- Track time spent and mistake category.

## Expected Outcome After 14 Days
- Master ~50–60 high ROI problems deeply.
- Recognize patterns within 2 minutes.
- Solve most mediums within ~25 minutes.
- Fluency in sliding window, linked list, tree DFS/BFS, monotonic stack, binary search, core DP, and backtracking.
